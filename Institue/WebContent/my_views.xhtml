To My Students

Section A

Chemistry is a vast subject with different fragrance in its every corner. Study in depth with proper concept is expected at the best effort in H.S / I.S.C / C.B.S.E level. All the competitive entrance exams including J.E.E (MAIN AND ADVANCED), A.I.P.M.T or all India Medical exams are based on concept and accuracy. So, each chapter of Chemistry should be studied thoroughly but in proper method.

Here is a question.What are the methods to study Modern Chemistry? It is not the way of Xerox of 40-50 pages in a made-easy note-book style (question answer pattern) for each chapter. It is not the way to study Chemistry in tick-mark style / or reading only suggestively. It is not the way to omit the chapters which you do not understand. Remember that,Chemistry is a technical subject, fully based on mathematics (not on bunch of made-easy notes).

Chemistry Is Fun!

As Chemistry includes different branches (Physical, Inorganic, Organic, Analytical etc.), you are to follow different techniques and methods which are suitable to them.If you study Chemistry without proper techniques and methods, you will not gather proper concept and will experience different sorts of problems throughout the whole course (especially during the exam-days).

Chemistry That Most Excellent Child of Intellect and Art.

During the course, students face many difficulties regarding Chemistry as they do not know the basics.They do not know how to use Road-maps and Charts when they read Inorganic and Organic Chemistry. They avoid the numerical problems in Physical Chemistry. From my vast teaching experience, I have noticed that most of the students do not have good skill in Mole concept, they avoid Volumetric calculation,Redox equilibrium, Problems on electrochemical cell etc.Organic chemistry,to many students is a night-mare experience due to wrong method of study. 

Another thing I have noticed that,few students get trapped and waste their valuable time regarding for-show practical in private tuition class in some cases.This proved to be fatal regarding conceptual angles necessary in JEE exams as well as in theoretical part of chemistry in board exams. In such circumstances you should know regularity in classes in your School will ensure good practical marks. Students leaving tuition from such places when join to my class, discover their weakness in physical chemistry, especially in organic chemistry. These two branches of chemistry carry huge marks not only in board exam but also in JEEs.Engg and Medical exams (JEEs) do not involve practical. 

So, dear students, read Chemistry from the very basic level.
